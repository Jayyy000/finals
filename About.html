About Us

VinTEAge Grills & Restaurant was established on August 08, 2021. It started as a milktea shop, that is why it was named Vin"TEA"ge. After couple of months, due to public demands, we started serving meals that will surely satisfy your cravings. We serve UNLIMITED chicken wings and rice, Chicken Inasal, Sizzling Pork Sisig, Sizzling Pork Dinuguan, mealbin Sizzling plates, Milktea, and a lot more. Come and visit us, and experienced Good Vibes at VinTEAge Grills & Restaurant!
